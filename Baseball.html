Top 10 Baseball Movies

Here at softball fanatics we love all sports on the diamond. From bilitz ball to wiffle ball we try to highlight it all with our weeklky list. This week's list we decided to focus on basball. This storied game has been played for well over a hundred years. And as American society has changed and evolved overtime so has this diamond sports. One of those evolutions is the game's promience on the big screen. Som without further a do here is our to 10 list of all-time baseball movies

Iowa farmer Ray Kinsella is inspired by a voice he can't ignore to pursue a dream he can hardly believe. Supported by his wife, Ray begins the quest by turning his ordinary cornfield into a place where dreams can come true.

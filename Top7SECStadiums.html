Top Seven SEC Stadiums

The Southeastern conference is more than what takes place on the dimond. It wouldn't be a true site about all things for this conference if was not in the fold. Even in the offseason football is king in the SEC becuase "It just means more". So with that being said we have made a list of the top seven SEC football stadiums.

1. Farout Field- University of Missouri

600 E Stadium Blvd
Columbia, MO
65201
(573) 882-6501

For more infomation about Farout Field visit mutigers.com

How about a cheer for ole Mizzou. Farout Field comes in at number one on this list. It may not be the biggest venue but when the Missouri Tigers are rolling the fans pack the hill and the enviroment is electric. When Tiger fans show out it becomes a diffcult place for opposing offesnes to operate as the sound can get defining, espcially when Mr. Brightside plays. An underated site witha frenzied fanbase.

2. Tiger Stadium- Louisiana State University

North Stadium Drive at Nicholson Drive, LSU Campus
Baton Rouge, LA
70803
(225) 578-2184

For more information about Tiger Stadium visit lsusports.net

When the sun goes down and the lights turn on at Tiger stadium its where dreams go to die. Better known as Death Valley, this stadium's atmosphere is best in primetime games. The fans get more frienzed and the Bayou Bengal Tigers get more amped as the opposing team looks to march into the valley of death.

3. Sanford Stadium- University of Georgia

100 Sanford Dr
Athens, GA
30602
(706) 542-9036

For more information about Sanford Stadium visit ugastadium.com

Hunker down for the silver britiches or you'll be barked at. Home of the Goergia Bulldogs Sanford Stadium hold the record for the loudest college stadium in the SEC. The Georgia faithful make this a diffcult place to play as their Bulldogs have a 25 home game winning streak.

4. Jordan Hare Stadium- Auburn University

251 S Donahue Dr
Auburn, AL
36849
(334) 844-4750

For more information about Jordan Hare Stadum visit auburntigers.com

Where cheers for "War Eagle" can be heard. On the plains in Auburn, Alabama this place is known for voodoo magic. It doesn;t matter the record the Auburn Tigers have if they at home best believe a team will be on its heels. There is always an external force at Jordan Hare that makes it that much more diffuclt for the enemy of the Auburn Tigers.

5. Bryant-Denny Stadium- University of Alabama

920 Paul W Bryant Dr
Tuscaloosa, AL
35401
(205) 348-2262

For more information about Bryan-Denny stadium visit rolltide.com

Roll Tide Roll. This site is filled with history as coaches like Paul "Bear" Bryant and Nick Saban have roamed its sidelines. But history isn't the only thing Bryant-Denny Stadium has, it has a tradition of excellence. The Crimson Tide have not lost a home since 2019. With that sucess and combined history it puts this site at number five on this list.

6. Ben Hill Griffin Stadium- University if Florida

157 Gale Lemerand Dr
Gainesville, FL
32611
(352) 375-4683

For more information about Ben Hill Griffin Stadium visit flordiagators.com

Welcome to the Swamp. The Flordia Gators sport a tough site in the SEC to play at. From dominant defenses to high flying offenses Flordia fans have seen it all from their Gators at Ben Hill Griffin. And when the sky turns dark and "I Won't Back Down," plays you better watch out if you're the opposing team.

7. Neyland Stadium- University of Tennessee

1300 Phillip Fulmer Way
Knoxville, TN
37916
(865) 974-1205

For more information about Neyland Stadium visit utsports.com

Good ole Rocky Top. On football Saturdays in east Tennessee over hundred thousand Volunterr fans will pack Neyland to support thier Vols. Its one of the largest sites in the nation and hen they Vols are good it makes it tough for opposing teams to come in and take a win.
